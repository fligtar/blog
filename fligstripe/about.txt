<p class="intro">I opened my first IHOP franchise at age 12, once rode a bucking Giant Seahorse for 7.6 seconds, and currently work on improving the <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox">Firefox add-ons</a> experience at <a href="http://www.mozilla.com">Mozilla</a>. At least one of these facts is probably not a lie.</p>

<h2>a little about me</h2>
<p class="notop">I grew up in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and acquired the nickname &#147;fligtar&#148; after my first character's name in the <abbr title="Massively Multiplayer Online Roleplaying Game">MMORPG</abbr> <a href="http://www.everquest.com">EverQuest</a> back in 2001.</p>

<p>I started using Firefox in 2004 and shortly afterwards wrote my first extension. I began volunteering with the <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org">Mozilla Add-ons</a> website in early 2006, with no idea it would eventually become my full-time job. I worked for Mozilla part-time as a web developer while attending <a href="http://www.lsu.edu">Louisiana State University</a> and <a href="http://i.fligtar.com/zombie-transcript.png">learning about zombies</a>. After graduation in 2008, I made the move to the San Francisco Bay Area so that I could be made fun of for my pronunciation of &#145;pen&#146; and using words like &#145;putt putt&#146; instead of &#145;minigolf&#146; and &#145;fixing&#146; instead of &#145;getting ready&#146;.</p>

<p>As Add-ons Product Manager, I try to make add-ons easier to discover, use, and create. Along with the rest of the add-ons team, I work on the <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org">add-ons gallery</a>, Firefox's Add-ons Manager, and other special projects.</p>

<h2>things I've made</h2>
<p class="notop">I started coding in middle school, so I've managed to rack up a lot of personal projects, some still in use and others that never saw the light of day. A couple that I still try to make time for are listed below.</p>

<dl>
    <dt><a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/addon/2848?src=external-blogabout">Password Exporter</a></dt>
    <dd>An add-on for Firefox and Thunderbird that can export and import your saved usernames and passwords for transferring between computers or backing up. More than 1.9 million downloads and 220,000 daily users.</dd>
    <dt><a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/addon/2140?src=external-blogabout">FavLoc</a></dt>
    <dd>An add-on for Firefox that adds custom save locations to the context menu, file menu, and download dialog. More than 97,000 downloads and 8,000 daily users.</dd>
</dl>
