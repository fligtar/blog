<p class="intro chrome">Hi! My name is <strong>Justin Scott</strong>, and I work on improving the <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox" class="subtle">add-ons</a> experience at <a href="http://www.mozilla.com" class="subtle">Mozilla</a>. I usually blog about Firefox add-ons and web development.</p>

<h2>a little about me</h2>
<p class="notop">I grew up in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and acquired the nickname "fligtar" after my first character's name in the <abbr title="Massively Multiplayer Online Roleplaying Game">MMORPG</abbr> <a href="http://www.everquest.com">EverQuest</a> back in 2001.</p>

<p>I started using Firefox in 2004 and shortly afterwards wrote my first extension. I began volunteering with the <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org">Mozilla Add-ons</a> website in early 2006, with no idea it would eventually become my full-time job. I worked for Mozilla part-time while attending <a href="http://www.lsu.edu">Louisiana State University</a> and <a href="http://i.fligtar.com/zombie-transcript.png">learning about zombies</a>. After graduation in late 2008, I made the move to the San Francisco Bay Area so that I could be made fun of for my pronunication of <em>pen</em> and using words like <em>putt putt</em> instead of minigolf and <em>fixing</em> instead of getting ready.</p>

<p>I care a lot about details. As Add-ons Product Manager, I primarily work on the <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org">add-ons website</a> and other special projects with the rest of the add-ons team to keep Firefox the most customizable browser with the largest selection of add-ons built by thousands of community members who love Firefox.</p>

<h2>things I've made</h2>
<p class="notop">I started coding in middle school, so I've managed to rack up a lot of personal projects, some still in use and others that never saw the light of day. A couple that I still try to make time for are listed below.</p>

<dl>
    <dt><a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/addon/2848?src=external-blogabout">Password Exporter</a></dt>
    <dd>An add-on for Firefox and Thunderbird that can export and import your saved usernames and passwords for transferring between computers or backing up. More than 1.4 million downloads and 220,000 daily users.</dd>
    <dt><a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/addon/2140?src=external-blogabout">FavLoc</a></dt>
    <dd>An add-on for Firefox that adds custom save locations to the context menu, file menu, and download dialog. More than 85,000 downloads and 8,000 daily users.</dd>
</dl>
